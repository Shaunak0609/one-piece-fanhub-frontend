Tony Tony Chopper

Bounty: 1,000 Berries

The doctor and the crew’s adorable reindeer

Tony Tony Chopper is the Straw Hat Pirates’ doctor — a blue-nosed reindeer who gained human traits after eating the Human-Human Fruit. Curious and kindhearted, Chopper treats every member of the crew with care, tending to their wounds and studying medicine to one day cure any disease. He can transform into multiple forms using his Rumble Ball invention, combining cuteness with surprising combat power. Though he’s often mistaken for the crew’s pet, Chopper is one of the Straw Hats’ most valuable members.
