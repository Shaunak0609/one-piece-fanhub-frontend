Charlotte Linlin (Big Mom)

Bounty: 4,388,000,000 Berries

The gluttonous Emperor who rules through fear

Big Mom, one of the Four Emperors, is the captain of the Big Mom Pirates and ruler of Totto Land. With her Soul-Soul Fruit powers, she manipulates souls to create living homies. Driven by gluttony and ambition, she dreams of a world where all races coexist — under her terrifying rule.
