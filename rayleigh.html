Silvers Rayleigh

Former First Mate of the Roger Pirates

The Dark King and mentor to the Pirate King’s successor

Silvers Rayleigh, the “Dark King,” was the first mate of the legendary Roger Pirates and one of the most respected figures in history. After the crew’s disbandment, he retired to Sabaody Archipelago, living a quiet life until he met Luffy. Rayleigh trained Luffy in Haki and taught him the foundations of true strength. Wise, humble, and immensely powerful, Rayleigh embodies the legacy of the Pirate King’s era.
