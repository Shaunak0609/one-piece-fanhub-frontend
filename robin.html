Nico Robin

Bounty: 930,000,000 Berries

The archaeologist who can read the true history

Nico Robin is the Straw Hat Pirates’ archaeologist. She ate the Flower-Flower Fruit, allowing her to sprout limbs anywhere. Her dream is to uncover the true history of the world by deciphering the Poneglyphs (being one of the only people who can read them). Calm, intelligent, and mysterious, she is the crew’s key to uncovering the secrets of the Void Century.
