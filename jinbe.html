Jinbe

Bounty: 1,100,000,000 Berries

The helmsman and honorable knight of the sea

Jinbe is the Straw Hat Pirates’ helmsman and a master of Fish-Man Karate. Once a Warlord of the Sea, he joined Luffy’s crew to help bring peace between humans and fish-men. Wise, calm, and immensely powerful, Jinbe is both a mentor and protector to his crewmates.
