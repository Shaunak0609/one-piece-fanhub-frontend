Vinsmoke Sanji

Bounty: 1,032,000,000 Berries

The cook who fights with his legs of fire

Sanji is the Straw Hat Pirates’ cook and one of the crew’s most skilled fighters. Trained under Zeff at the Baratie restaurant, Sanji is a culinary genius who dreams of finding the All Blue — the ocean where every fish in the world can be found. He fights using powerful kicks infused with fiery Diable Jambe techniques, never allowing his hands — the tools of a chef — to be harmed. Sanji is chivalrous, loyal, and always ready to protect his crewmates.
