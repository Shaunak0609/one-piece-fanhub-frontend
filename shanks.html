Red-Haired Shanks

Bounty: 4,048,900,000 Berries

The Emperor who inspired Luffy’s dream

Shanks is the captain of the Red-Haired Pirates and one of the Four Emperors. Once an apprentice on Gol D. Roger’s ship, he passed his straw hat to a young Luffy — inspiring his journey to become Pirate King. Calm and powerful, Shanks commands respect across the seas. Recently, he was discovered to be a celestial dragon with a twin brother who currently leads the God Knights
