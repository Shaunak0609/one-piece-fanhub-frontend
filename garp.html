Monkey D. Garp

Vice Admiral, Hero of the Marines

The legendary Marine and Luffy’s grandfather

Garp is a Marine hero renowned for his incredible strength and countless victories against pirates, including Gol D. Roger himself and Rocks D. Xebec. Despite his strict sense of justice, he cares deeply for his family — even when their dreams clash with his duties. Known as “Garp the Fist,” he stands as one of the Marines’ strongest figures.
