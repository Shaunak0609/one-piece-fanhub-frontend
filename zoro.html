Roronoa Zoro

Bounty: 1,111,000,000 Berries

The swordsman who aims to be the world’s strongest

Roronoa Zoro is the swordsman of the Straw Hat Pirates and Luffy’s first crewmate. Known for his Three-Sword Style, Zoro dreams of becoming the world’s greatest swordsman. Despite his poor sense of direction, his loyalty and strength make him a core member of the crew.
