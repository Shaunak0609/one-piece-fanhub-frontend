Akainu (Sakazuki)

Fleet Admiral of the Marines

The man of absolute justice

Akainu is the current Fleet Admiral and an uncompromising believer in absolute justice. Possessing the powers of the Magma-Magma Fruit, he can melt anything in his path. His ruthless decisions during the Marineford War cemented him as one of the most feared figures in the Marines.
