Franky

Bounty: 394,000,000 Berries

The shipwright and cyborg with a SUPER personality

Franky is the Straw Hat Pirates’ shipwright and a powerful cyborg. After building the Thousand Sunny, he joined the crew to sail the seas on his dream ship. Loud, energetic, and inventive, Franky combines engineering genius with unmatched strength, fighting using his mechanical body and strong cola-powered weapons.
