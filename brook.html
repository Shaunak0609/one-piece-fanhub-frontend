Brook

Bounty: 383,000,000 Berries

The living musician who came back from the dead

Brook is the Straw Hat Pirates’ musician and a living skeleton revived by the Revive-Revive Fruit. Despite his spooky appearance, he’s cheerful and polite, always ready to play a song to lift spirits. Brook dreams of reuniting with Laboon, the whale he promised to return to decades ago.
