Kizaru (Borsalino)

Admiral of the Marines

The man of light who moves at the speed of light

Kizaru is a Marine Admiral known for his calm and carefree demeanor, yet his power is terrifying — granted by the Glint-Glint Fruit, which allows him to move and attack at light speed. His laziness contrasts his deadly potential, making him an unpredictable force of the Marines.
