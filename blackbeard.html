Marshall D. Teach (Blackbeard)

Bounty: 3,996,000,000 Berries

The only man with the power of two Devil Fruits

Blackbeard is one of the Four Emperors and captain of the Blackbeard Pirates. Once a member of Whitebeard’s crew, he betrayed his comrades to claim the Dark-Dark Fruit. Ambitious and cunning, Blackbeard became the only person known to wield two Devil Fruit powers, making him one of the most dangerous men alive.
