Kaido

Bounty: 4,611,100,000 Berries

The Strongest Creature in the World

Kaido, one of the Four Emperors, was the captain of the Beasts Pirates and sought to start the greatest war in history. With his Dragon Devil Fruit, he could transform into a massive Eastern dragon. Kaido’s obsession with strength and his near-immortality made him one of the most feared beings in One Piece.
