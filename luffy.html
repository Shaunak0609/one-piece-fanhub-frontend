Monkey D. Luffy

Bounty: 3,000,000,000 Berries

The man who wants to be pirate king: the pinnacle of freedom

Monkey D. Luffy is the captain of the Straw Hat Pirates and the main protagonist of One Piece. He ate the Gum-Gum Fruit, giving him a rubber body that he uses in combat with creativity and humor. His dream is to become the Pirate King by finding the legendary treasure, the One Piece. Despite his carefree nature, Luffy is fiercely loyal to his friends and will go to any length to protect them.
