Donquixote Doflamingo

Bounty: 340,000,000 Berries

The Heavenly Demon and ruthless king of Dressrosa

Doflamingo, known as the Heavenly Demon, was a Warlord of the Sea and ruler of Dressrosa. With his String-String Fruit powers, he manipulates both people and nations alike. A cruel schemer and former Celestial Dragon, Doflamingo’s ambition and charisma made him one of the most dangerous figures in the New World.
