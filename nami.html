Nami

Bounty: 366,000,000 Berries

The cat burglar and master navigator

Nami is the navigator of the Straw Hat Pirates, known for her intelligence and ability to chart safe courses through even the most dangerous seas. She dreams of creating a complete map of the world. With her Clima-Tact weapon and her pet cloud Zeus, she controls the weather in battle.
