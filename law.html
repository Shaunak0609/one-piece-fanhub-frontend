Trafalgar D. Water Law

Bounty: 3,000,000,000 Berries

The Surgeon of Death

Trafalgar Law is the captain and doctor of the Heart Pirates. A brilliant strategist and skilled fighter, he wields the powers of the Op-Op Fruit, allowing him to manipulate anything within his “Room.” Law is calm, intelligent, and often takes on the role of Luffy’s reluctant ally, sharing his ambition to overthrow the oppressive powers of the world. Despite his grim past, Law fights for a better future.
